The Pipeline

One idea enters. A living company exits. Eight autonomous stages.

01

Board Decision

Six AI executives debate your idea. GPT-4o crafts strategy, Gemini fact-checks every claim, Claude Opus makes the final GO/NO-GO call. Multi-model reasoning eliminates blind spots.

02

Team Auto-Build

Education team designs curriculum with Opus, collects world-class knowledge via Perplexity, and generates specialized agent code. Zero manual setup.

03

Design Router

Automatically determines 3D level, style family, and pulls from 59 enterprise design systems. Stripe, Linear, Notion — live references for every project.

04

Reference Analyzer

Scans 16 component repos — 726 cards, 180 forms, 14 shaders. Matches project type to optimal UI patterns and animations.

05

Full-Stack Build

FE and BE agents work in parallel with git worktree isolation. Stitch handles UI components. GLSL shaders render 3D heroes. nano-banana generates visuals.

06

Visual QA Loop

Playwright screenshots every page. Gemini Vision scores against design specs. Below 85% triggers automatic redesign — up to 3 iterations until it's perfect.

07

Deploy & Launch

Vercel deployment. Marketing team pre-generates all content during the build phase. Launch day: everything is already live and running.

08

Operations Loop

Daily: Instagram captions, blog posts, sales DMs. Weekly: performance review, strategy pivots. The company operates itself 24/7, forever.
